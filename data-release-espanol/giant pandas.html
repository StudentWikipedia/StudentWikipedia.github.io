What they look like:

Pandas are famous for their black-and-white fur. Scientists think this helps them hide in the snow (the white part) and in the shadows of the forest (the black part). They also have a "pseudo-thumb" which is actually an enlarged wrist bone that helps them grip bamboo stalks!


Facts for nerds!

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Status: Vulnerable
  • Lifespan: 20 years in the wild
  • Daily Food: 26 to 84 lbs of bamboo
  1. A newborn panda is about the size of a stick of butter.
  2. They spend about 12 hours a day just eating.
  3. Unlike other bears, pandas do not hibernate in the winter.
